FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Does the body scanner need special hardware?

No. Thrive uses your phone camera with guided poses. Some devices support on‑device processing for privacy.

Can I switch between Basic and Premium?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Your data stays with you.

Is my health data secure?

We encrypt data in transit and at rest. Camera scans can be processed locally when supported.
